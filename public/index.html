About us

Embarking on a mission fueled by the spirit of giving, RIP CHEEMS is not just a crypto token – it's a movement. Our aim is to raise funds and support for other Cheems-like initiatives, mirroring the success of Dogecoin's charitable endeavors. With RIP CHEEMS, we're not only embracing the fun and creativity of internet culture but also channeling its power to make a positive impact. By investing in RIP CHEEMS, you're not only becoming a part of a vibrant crypto community but also contributing to a brighter future for projects that share the same spirit of generosity and goodwill.

Tokenomics

Initial Distribution

- 50% allocated to community rewards and incentives.
- 20% allocated to development and project maintenance.
- 15% allocated to liquidity provision.
- 10% allocated to marketing and partnerships.
- 5% allocated to the founding team and advisors.

Staking and Governance

- Holders can stake RC to earn additional rewards and participate in governance decisions.
- Governance proposals can be submitted and voted on by stakers.

Utility & Liquidity

- RC can be used for transaction fees, access to exclusive features, and in-app purchases within the Rip Cheems ecosystem.
- A portion of the initial supply is allocated to provide liquidity on decentralized exchanges to ensure trading availability.

Burning Mechanism & Vesting

- A deflationary mechanism is implemented where a small percentage of each transaction fee is burned, reducing the total supply over time.
- Team and advisor tokens are subject to vesting periods to ensure long-term commitment to the project.

Token Release Schedule & Security

- The team and advisor tokens are released gradually over a multi-year period to prevent any sudden sell-offs.
- Smart contracts undergo regular security audits to protect the token and user assets.

Listed on & Liquidity

- Listed on Binance Smart Chain
- Regular updates, AMAs, and community events to foster engagement and transparency.
